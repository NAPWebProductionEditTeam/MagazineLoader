Demure and irreverent, the French actress is the epitome of modern chic

"I love it when clothes tell a story and I like finding pieces that move me," says Poésy of her affinity with fashion. Mixing easy jersey basics, sleek denim and luxurious tweed jackets works for weekends, while at night she opts for elegant midi-length dresses and ladylike heels. Minimal makeup and pared-back jewelry complete the look.
